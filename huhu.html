About Timoga Springs

A hidden gem in Northern Mindanao

Timoga Springs is a collection of natural spring water pools located in Barangay Buru-un, Iligan City. Known for its crystal-clear, cold waters that flow directly from the mountains, Timoga offers a refreshing escape from the tropical heat.

The springs feature several privately-owned pools with varying depths, perfect for both children and adults. The water maintains a cool temperature year-round, making it an ideal destination no matter the season.

How to Get There

  • From Iligan City proper, take a jeepney bound for Buru-un (15-20 minute ride)
  • By private vehicle: Follow the national highway towards Buru-un, approximately 10km from city center
  • Look for signage along the highway indicating the entrance to Timoga Springs
